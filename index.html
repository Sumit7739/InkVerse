Beneath a smile and careless charm, a mind sharper than any blade moves in silence. The Lover’s Gambit follows Ariv — a boy the world dismisses, yet a grandmaster hidden in plain sight — as he navigates a ruthless game of love, loyalty, betrayal, and power. Every move is calculated. Every kindness, a strategy. Every love, a battlefield. In the end, there will be only one victor and he has already chosen his Queen.
